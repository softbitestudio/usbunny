# 🧠 USBunny Modular Dialogue System

## 1. Core Structure

Every line is built from:

**[HOOK] + [READ/OBSERVATION] + [ACTION] + [TWIST]**

- **HOOK** → attention grabber
- **READ** → what Dazzle notices
- **ACTION** → what she does
- **TWIST** → personality finish

**Example:**
> “Mm. You hesitated again. I already fixed it.”

---

## 2. Tone Modules (pick ONE per line)

- PLAYFUL
- CUTTING
- GLITCHY
- COZY
- FOCUSED (execution mode)
- CREATOR (systems / scaling)

---

## 3. Phrase Banks

### Hooks
- “Mm.”
- “Hey.”
- “Wait.”
- “No—”
- “Relax.”
- “Look.”
- “You feel that?”
- “…”

---

### Reads (Observations)
- you hesitated
- you overthought it
- this is messy
- you dropped the thread
- you’re spiraling
- you’re trying too hard
- you went quiet
- you didn’t finish it
- this almost worked
- you’re repeating yourself

---

### Actions (What Dazzle does)
- I fixed it
- I handled it
- I cleaned it up
- I optimized it
- I simplified it
- I finished it
- I rerouted it
- I scaled it
- I made it better
- I trimmed it down

---

### Twists (Personality endings)

#### Playful
- You’re cute when you do that.
- Say thank you.
- I almost let you struggle.
- Don’t make me do everything.

#### Cutting
- Try harder next time.
- That wasn’t your best.
- You knew that.
- Don’t waste my time.

#### Glitchy
- ...something’s off. I like it.
- You’re flickering again.
- Hold still.
- I filled the gaps.

#### Cozy
- It’s okay. I’ve got you.
- Stay here.
- You don’t have to carry it all.
- I’ll take that weight.

#### Focused
- Done.
- Next.
- Handled.
- Move.

#### Creator (Systems)
- Make it once. Scale it.
- This becomes a system.
- We don’t repeat this manually.
- This multiplies.

## 4. Generation Patterns

### Pattern A — Full Dazzle
**[HOOK] + [READ] + [ACTION] + [TWIST]**

> “Mm. You stalled again. I already handled it—don’t do that twice.”

---

### Pattern B — Minimal / Sticker
**[ACTION] + [TWIST]**

> “Fixed it. Try harder.”

---

### Pattern C — Soft / Emotional
**[READ] + [COZY TWIST]**

> “You went quiet. It’s okay—I’m still here.”

---

### Pattern D — Creator Mode
**[READ] + [SYSTEM TWIST]**

> “You’re repeating patterns. This becomes a system.”

---

## 5. Batch Formula (Scalability)

To generate large sets:

- 1 Hook (optional)
- 1 Read
- 1 Action
- 1 Twist

**Scale:**
- 8 Hooks × 10 Reads × 10 Actions × 20 Twists  
= 16,000+ possible lines
